About the game

EAFC 25 is a free to play mobile game.
It is a football game where u can play different competitions with ur favorate team,
create ur own team and play division rivals against other players to get the highest rank
and complete quests so u can get gems for opening packs and maybe get a great player for your team
or coins so u can buy a player u want in your team. The goal of the game is to create the best team so that u can compete with others and potencially rank up.
U can also obtain players by completing live events like chronicalls, TOTY events(to get team of the year players)
or chronicalls(a lot of skill games and matches against an AI team to get a great player)
In this game there are retired football players and football players that are playing right now.
The retired players who were good and were legends are called icons and the not so good retired players are heroes.
And the currently playing players get a base card where the game actually rates their football skills and gives them a rating between 53 and 99 And they could have a TOTY(team of the year) card where its rating is 109.
How higher rated the players in your team are, the higher your team is rated and performs. And there a lots of different cards for the currently playing players.
the ratings go from 53 to 114 and team ratings 55 to 116.
You can also pay to get the best players.
U can purchase FCponts. With FCpoints, u can buy coins and go to the market and purchase the best players.
Or gems where u can afford the best players with.
